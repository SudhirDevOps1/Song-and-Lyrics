Welcome to **SongVibe Ultimate Pro**! A professional, aesthetic, and feature-rich browser-based Lyrics & Reel Maker. 
With SongVibe, you can instantly create Instagram/TikTok ready lyric videos directly in your browser.

## 🚀 Key Highlights

- **Cinematic Lyrics**: Word-by-word typewriter animations, glowing text, and multiple fonts.
- **Custom Aesthetic Backgrounds**: Upload any image or video (looping) to use as your reel's background.
- **In-Browser Export**: Direct screen-recording of the canvas, downloaded as a `.webm` file without external software.
- **Hybrid Playback**: Supports both YouTube Links and Local Audio files (`.mp3` / `.wav`).
- **Responsive & Smart**: Locks into a 9:16 mobile aspect ratio automatically for perfect Reel cropping.

## 🎛️ How to Use

1. **Add Media**: Paste a YouTube Link OR Upload a Local Audio File.
2. **Edit Lyrics**: Paste your lyrics in the `[0:00] Text` format in the right sidebar.
3. **Customize Design**:
   - Choose a Theme (Neon, Snow, Rose, Gold).
   - Upload a Custom Background.
   - Set Lyric Size, Glow Intensity, and Alignment.
4. **Enter Reel Mode**: Click the **Reel Mode** button in the header.
5. **Record**: Hit the **Record Reel** button, play the song, and download your masterpiece!
