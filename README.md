## 📸 Visual Guide & How to Use

### 1️⃣ How to Add a Song (YouTube or Local Audio)
**Where to paste the song link?**
Look at the **Left Sidebar** under the **"ADD MEDIA"** section. 
- **YouTube Link:** Paste any YouTube video link in the text box that says `"YouTube Link Paste..."` and click the **`+`** (Plus) button.
- **Local Audio:** Click on **`Upload Local MP3 / Audio`** to select an MP3 file directly from your computer or phone.

> *Caption: The Add Media panel on the left sidebar where you paste YouTube links or upload MP3s.*

### 2️⃣ How to Extract Lyrics from an Image & Paste Them
If you have a screenshot of lyrics and don't want to type them manually:
1. **Extract Text:** Use **Google Lens** (on Android/Web) or **Live Text** (on iPhone). Just open the image, highlight the text, and click "Copy".
2. **Format Time:** Before pasting, ensure your lyrics have timestamps at the beginning of each line.
   *Example:*
   ```text
   [0:15.0] First line of the song
   [0:18.5] Second line here
   
   [0:25.0] Next stanza...
   ```
   *(Note: If you leave an empty line between lines, the system will automatically calculate the perfect timestamp to clear the screen!)*
3. **Paste:** Go to the **Right Sidebar** (Lyrics Editor), paste your extracted lyrics into the large **"LYRICS"** text box, and click **"Apply Changes"**.

> *Caption: The Lyrics Editor on the right sidebar where you paste your time-synced lyrics.*

### 3️⃣ How to Use the 7-Color "Rainbow" Feature & Custom Colors
- **Global Rainbow Theme:** In the left sidebar, click the **"Rainbow"** pill button under the Theme section. All your lyrics will flow with a beautiful 7-color gradient!
- **Specific Line Color:** If you want just ONE line to be a specific color, add a color tag before the text in the Lyrics Editor.
   *Example:*
   ```text
   [0:15.0] [color:#ff0000] This line will be pure red!
   [0:18.0] [color:gold] This line will be glowing gold!
   ```

### 4️⃣ How to Export & Record a Video (Fullscreen Mode)
Don't use third-party tools! Use the built-in Reel Maker:
1. Set up your song, lyrics, font, and background (upload a custom background from the left sidebar if you want).
2. Look at the **Main Lyrics Box** in the center of the screen. In its top-right corner, there is a **Fullscreen Button [🔲]**.
3. Click it! The entire player (with the particles and active lyrics) will expand to fill your screen.
4. Use a screen recorder (like OBS, Windows Game Bar, or your phone's screen recorder) to capture the video perfectly without any sidebars!
